About Space

Space, the final frontier, is a vast expanse of wonder and mystery. It encompasses everything beyond Earth's atmosphere, including planets, stars, galaxies, and the unknown depths of the universe.

Fascinating Space Facts

  • There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on Earth.
  • The Sun accounts for 99.86% of the mass in our solar system.
  • One day on Venus is longer than one year on Earth.
